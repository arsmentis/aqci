Oakland County’s Source for Complete, Top-to-Bottom Industrial & Commercial Cleaning Services

American Quality Cleaning, Inc., one of the most trusted commercial cleaning companies in Oakland County Michigan, is ready to provide all of your business cleaning needs.

American Quality Cleaning has a sparkling reputation as an exceptional 'green' cleaning company that is both dependable and affordable. The staff is committed to meeting and exceeding client expectations, and our quality assurance process ensures that we never miss a spot!

When it comes to interior or exterior cleaning of your commercial property, office building, medical facility, municipal building, restaurant, or industrial facility, American Quality Cleaning, Inc. of Waterford, MI is committed to earning and keeping your business.

American Quality Cleaning’s janitorial service professionals are fully trained in safe and compliant commercial cleaning products and practices, including germicides and green products. Additionally, the techniques employed by our staff include the highest industry standards and can be tailored for your satisfaction.

Carpet & Upholstery Cleaning

The professional cleaners at American Quality Cleaning have industry certifications, which means they have undergone extensive training on mold prevention, stain removal, and how to use professional carpet cleaning equipment. They have expert knowledge about carpet and upholstery construction, such as the type of backing in the carpet, the types of fibers, and which cleaning methods and products are most effective for each type of carpet and upholstery. Also, they can recommend which carpet cleaning products and methods are suitable for your needs.

New Construction Cleanup

Construction and post-construction cleanup is a time-consuming task that requires specialized knowledge and tools to get the result you want. There is more to construction cleaning than just removing debris, dirt, and dust. It is crucial to use the right cleaning products to achieve quality results without compromising the quality of the finishes. The professional cleaners at American Quality Cleaning have procedures and methods in place that allow the job to be done quickly, and with just the right amount of cleaning products, which helps keeps costs low.
