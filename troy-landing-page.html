Troy, MI - Upscale, Big-City Offerings in a Midsize City

Troy brings together a unique blend of upscale offerings, business opportunities, and city services. With more than 80,000 diverse residents, Troy is Oakland County’s largest city. Businesses of all sizes and offerings make a home in Troy - from small, local shops to large-scale, international companies, you will find assorted options here. The nation continues to take note of Troy’s safety, education, places to raise a family, and more in national evaluations such as Niche Rankings and 24/7 Wall Street.

Locals and visitors find everything they could need or want at shopping centers in Troy. The luxury offerings at Somerset Collection mall from over 170 shops set within a beautiful, award-winning layout spanning North and South locations connected by a climate-controlled sky bridge. Or, the two-story indoor super regional Oakland Mall offers over 100 stores of affordable goods.

Troy is a city grounded in the present but also proud of its rich history. Take an immersive trip back to the 1800s and early 1900s to see, experience, and learn from Michigan’s rural heritage at the Troy Historic Village. Hear stories of a rustic life while you stand in an authentic log cabin, brick one-room school, wagon shop, and blacksmith’s forge.

Education in Troy extends far beyond history with its diverse institutions of higher learning. People expand their opportunities at such varied places as Walsh College, the Motion Picture Institute, the Michigan College of Beauty, Michigan State University’s Eli Broad College of Business, and Chamberlain University College of Nursing.

The staff of American Quality Cleaning live and work throughout Oakland County. As a close neighbor, we can quickly come to your place of business in Troy and provide our expert janitorial services.
