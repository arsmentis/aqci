Carpet & Upholstery Cleaning

Commercial Carpet Cleaning Services in Oakland County, MI 

In addition to regular deep cleaning for your carpet and upholstery, American Quality Cleaning utilizes advanced hot water extraction methods for exceptional results, offers cleaning, stripping and waxing for vinyl, tile and other hard flooring.

Clean Carpets and Floors Means Less Illness at Your Workplace

A dirty carpet not only looks ugly, but it also poses health risks for you and other people around you. Carpets can accumulate allergens that in time can affect you and your coworker’s health.

Pollen and dirt can be trapped in the carpet and spread into the atmosphere each time you walk on it or vacuum it. Professional carpet cleaning is one of the leading ways to reduce employee sick days and general office discomfort.

Extensive Knowledge and Experience in Commercial Cleaning Methods

The professional cleaners at American Quality Cleaning of Waterford have industry certifications, which means they have undergone training on mold prevention, stain removal, and how to use professional tools. They have expert knowledge about carpet and upholstery construction, such as the type of backing in the carpet, the types of fibers, and which cleaning methods and products are most effective for each type of carpet and upholstery. Also, they can recommend which carpet cleaning products and methods are suitable for your needs.

We Use Top-of-the-Line Equipment to Get Exceptional Results

Do-it-yourself carpet cleaning machines are not as effective as professional carpet & upholstery cleaning machines when it comes to eliminating blemishes and soiling. Using a machine and trying to eliminate stains from an expensive carpet and upholstery can be a catastrophic endeavor. The possible damage includes discoloration, contraction, permanent staining, and persistent odors due to excess moisture.

The powerful vacuums utilized at American Quality Cleaning eliminate deeply trapped allergens and dirt that reside within your carpets and upholstery. After thoroughly washing them, they employ high-powered extractors to get rid of excess moisture to accelerate the drying process. Doing this hampers the growth of mold and mildew. Our upholstery cleaning specialists employ the right machinery, have the knowledge needed to use them correctly, and they have cleaning and pre-treatment solutions that you cannot obtain over-the-counter.
