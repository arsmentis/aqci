Window Cleaning

Commercial Window Cleaning Services for Your Office, Medical Clinic, or Municipal Building in Waterford, MI

When it comes to commercial window cleaning, choose a professional service that won’t leave streaks or damage your windows.

Clean windows improve the amount of natural light, offer a nice view, and can improve any working environment.

However, over time, debris and dirt on your windows can reduce the attractiveness of your building, as well as the positive effect sunlight has on your workplace and employee morale.

Additionally, a buildup of dirt and debris on your windows can cause permanent damage if not addressed regularly. Studies have even shown that dirty windows result in lower sales and customers having a poor perception of your business!

It can be a huge hassle keeping your windows clean, especially with Michigan’s unpredictable weather. With American Quality Cleaning as your commercial cleaning service, you can rest assured knowing your windows are always in good 'clean' hands.

Extend the Lifespan of Your Windows

Over time, debris and dirt build-up can get etched into the glass and cause permanent scratches and damage. These marks can weaken the panes and lead to chipping, cracks, and other costly damages in the future. By using special cleaning products and their extensive expertise, our commercial window cleaning experts can make your windows last longer.

Knowledge of Specialized Cleaning Methods Provides Improved Results

Different windows need special cleaning methods and cleaning solutions to obtain the best results. For instance, stained glass and tinted windows both need special treatment. Employing regular cleaning materials on such windows can damage them, leaving you with a potentially huge repair bill and an unattractive look. To ensure your windows are cleaned properly, leave this task to the professionals. 

Leave Window Cleaning to the Professionals!

Washing windows without the right experience and gear can be a dangerous endeavor. Even a fall from one story can give you serious injuries. If you employ the services of a professional window cleaner, you and your employees won't have to work with hazardous cleaning solutions, get up the ladder, or maneuver in risky areas trying to clean your windows. 

Clean Windows Provide Many Benefits

Clean windows allow more natural light to enter your building. Having a cleaner, brighter workspace provides several advantages, such as:

  • Better first impressions from clients and customers
  • Increased focus, which enhances employee performance
  • Improved energy efficiency
  • Enhanced curb appeal

Our professionals can effectively eliminate all streaks, smudges, and build-up of dirt on your windows.
