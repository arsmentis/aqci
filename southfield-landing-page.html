Southfield, MI - ‘The Center of it All’

With several significant highway connections and more than 10,000 business, it is easy to see how the city of Southfield lives up to its headline of being at “the center of it all.” These highways bring in enough working day visitors and employees that the city more than doubles its size of 70,000 residents.

Many of the city’s office and sales employees work at the landmark Southfield Town Center. The Center includes five skyscrapers, a convention center, and a Westin hotel. Often called the Golden Triangle, the Center catches the sun over Northwestern Highway creating a radiant golden glow. Continue down Northwestern Highway to find Park West Gallery, the largest privately-owned art gallery in the world.

Diversity is a cornerstone of Southfield’s identity expressed in the varied backgrounds of its residents and visitors and the rich educational opportunities. Southfield expresses its value for educational opportunities with its seven universities, colleges, and training centers, including Lawrence Technological University, Abcott Institute, The Specs Howard School of Media Arts, and Oakland Community College.

American Quality Cleaning calls Waterford home, and we are proud to support our close neighbor, Southfield. We meet you at your industrial or commercial location to ensure you never again have to say “you missed a spot.”
