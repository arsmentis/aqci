Bloomfield Township - A Close-Knit Community to Live and Work

The research company Niche named Bloomfield Township as the second best place to live in Michigan when looking at home values, generational and cultural diversity, and family-friendly living. The township draws its more than 40,000 residents together into a comfortable, safe community to live and work. As a charter township, a seven-member board of trustees governs Bloomfield (as locals refer to it) instead of a mayor.

The Township is mostly residential - 95% in fact! But it still embraces both local and national businesses. Several large corporations have moved to Bloomfield or recently expanded their presence in the township. Look for the classy shops and restaurants along Telegraph or Woodward Ave.

Bloomfield Township brings exciting events to its residents and the public. In 2019, Bloomfield Township Public Library will host movies that were part of the Italian Film Festival USA. National golf competitions such as the PGA Championship and the US Open continue to choose Bloomfield’s Oakland Hills Country Club as a site.

American Quality Cleaning joins the Bloomfield community as a business partner. When a Bloomfield business chooses to work with us, we come to you!
